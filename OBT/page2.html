Effects of Lead Poisoning

Lead is a highly toxic metal and a very strong poison. Lead poisoning is a serious and sometimes fatal condition and occurs when lead builds up in the body. Lead poisoning is more harmful to children because their brains and nervous systems are still developing. While poisoning can be treated, damage caused cannot be reversed.
